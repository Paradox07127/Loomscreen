# Third Party Notices

## WebGL rain glass renderer

The WebGL rain wallpaper renderer adapts the raindrop lifecycle, water-map
composition, refraction shader structure, and drop texture assets from:

- Codrops RainEffect by Lucas Bebber: https://github.com/codrops/RainEffect

The visual target also references:

- Steam Workshop "Drops" visual reference: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=955880074

No Steam Workshop assets or source files are bundled in LiveWallpaper.
